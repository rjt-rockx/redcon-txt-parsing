Armory

Upgrades improve durability and infantry body armor.

Fleet Command

Airships may travel back for repairs, to return wounded soldiers, bring reinforcements or to replenish a payload.

Kinetic Shield Core
Processors around the core track the incoming projectile and compute its exact point of entry into the hexagonal matrix, directing strong electromagnetic force against the impact. Resulting collision explodes the projectile, temporarily disintegrating the matrix.
Kinetic Shield stops 10 damage before disintegration.
Cannot stop beam weapons, guided missiles and airship bombs.
Any damage inflicted upon the core weakens the integrity of hexagonal matrix.
Three operators increase the speed of matrix restoration to 160%.
Fully operated Kinetic Shield stops up to 40 damage before disintegration.
